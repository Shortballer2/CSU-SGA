About SGA

The Student Government Association (SGA) serves as the liaison between students and university administration.

We advocate for student needs, host events that enrich campus life, and work to create a vibrant Buccaneer community.

Executive Officers

Our executive officers oversee all operations of the Student Government Association.

SGA Cabinet

The Cabinet assists the executives and leads key SGA initiatives throughout the year.

Senators

Senators represent each graduating class and advocate for the needs of the student body.
